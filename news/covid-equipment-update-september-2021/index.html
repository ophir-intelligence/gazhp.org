Update: Oxygen Concentrators, PPE and Equipment En Route to Zambia

12 September 2021

A round-up of the medical equipment and supplies GAZHP and our partners sourced, funded and shipped as Zambia battled the third wave of COVID-19.

Oxygen concentrators

  • Ten 10-litre oxygen concentrators purchased by GAZHP from China were delivered to the airline for freight, expected to arrive in Zambia within a week or two.
  • Ten 5-litre oxygen concentrators were sourced by GAZHP from Turkey, with freight support in advanced stages of being secured.

PPE from the UK diaspora

PPE donated by UK-based Zambians and shipped by GAZHP for handover to the Ministry of Health included:

  • 6,000 face shields
  • 20,000 surgical masks
  • 100 disposable gowns
  • 100 alcohol sanitisers
  • 1,000 aprons
  • 1,000 gloves

Pulse oximeters

200 pulse oximeters — donated by Lifebox after being contacted by GAZHP — were handed over, including 200 covers and 200 paediatric oxygen sensors.

Donations from Australia

Collected by GAZHP members in Australia and awaiting freight sponsored by Airlink:

  • 23 ventilators (with the possibility of 7 more)
  • 18 haemodialysis machines
  • 2 dialysis chairs
  • 6 defibrillators
