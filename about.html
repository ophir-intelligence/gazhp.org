About GAZHP

A global movement of Zambian healthcare professionals united by one purpose — to transform the health of a nation through collaboration, expertise, and love for Zambia.

Our Mission

Making a Lasting Impact on Zambia's Healthcare

Our mission is to make a lasting, positive impact on Zambia's healthcare system by driving innovation through technology, research, and continuous medical education.

We launch voluntary projects supporting vulnerable communities, and we aspire to become one of the most influential diaspora-led groups advancing healthcare delivery across Zambia. Every initiative is grounded in accountability, collaboration, and a deep commitment to sustainability — building a system that endures long after our programs end.

Our Vision

A Zambia where every citizen has access to world-class healthcare — delivered by Zambians.

We envision a future where Zambia's health system is strong, self-sufficient, and driven by a skilled domestic workforce supported by a vibrant global diaspora network.

Our Story

How GAZHP Came to Be

A global movement that started with one powerful question posed on a radio program in 2019.

March 2019

The Spark: Dr. David Kazhila — a Canadian-based cardiologist with 25+ years of experience — is asked on a Zambian radio program what physicians in the diaspora could contribute to Zambia. The question ignites a vision to mobilize healthcare professionals globally.

July 2019

The Partnership Begins: Dr. Kazhila meets with Dr. Sebastian Benaya in Canada to develop the concept. Together they envision establishing a modern hospital in Zambia offering services comparable to international facilities.

August 2019

Growing Fast: Outreach to colleagues expands the initiative rapidly. 20 doctors join within weeks, including Dr. John Musonda in South Africa and Dr. Lorita Kabwe, a Zambia-based cardiologist.

September 2019

Momentum Builds: Word spreads through networks of Zambian healthcare professionals worldwide. 40+ healthcare professionals are now actively involved in shaping the alliance.

December 2019

Officially Registered: GAZHP is formally registered as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization in Delaware, USA — establishing the legal and governance foundation for lasting impact.

2020 – Present

Programs Launch & Grow: GAZHP launches its core programs in mental health, cardiovascular care, and primary care — expanding membership across 15+ countries and deepening partnerships with Zambian healthcare institutions.

Core Programs

Our Areas of Focus

Three flagship programs, each designed to address a critical gap in Zambia's healthcare landscape.

🧠
Program 01
Addiction & Mental Health

GAZHP promotes mental health awareness and builds culturally relevant services through partnerships between diaspora professionals and local providers. We work to reduce stigma, train counselors, and connect communities with compassionate, accessible mental health care.

❤️
Program 02
Cardiovascular Care

Heart disease is a growing challenge in Zambia. We strengthen cardiovascular care through clinical training, specialist mentorship, telemedicine consultations, and partnerships with global cardiology experts — bringing advanced care to patients who need it most.

🏥
Program 03
Family Medicine & Primary Care

Strong primary care is the backbone of a healthy society. We support training, research, and international collaboration to build a robust primary healthcare workforce in Zambia — ensuring every community has access to skilled, compassionate care.

Our Values

The Principles That Guide Everything We Do

🤝 Collaboration

We believe in the power of collective action. By connecting diaspora professionals with local communities, we achieve more together than any individual could alone.

🔬 Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest professional and ethical standards — because Zambia's patients deserve nothing less than world-class care and expertise.

♻️ Sustainability

Every program is built for the long term. We prioritize capacity building over dependency, ensuring our impact outlasts our direct involvement.

💎 Integrity

As a registered nonprofit, we operate with full transparency and accountability to our members, donors, and the communities we serve across Zambia.
