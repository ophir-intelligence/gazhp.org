Our Team

Meet the Board of Directors — healthcare and finance professionals across the diaspora and in Zambia, united by one mission: transforming healthcare in Zambia.

Leadership

Board of Directors

The board members driving GAZHP's strategy, governance and vision.

Dr. David Gordon Kazhila
Co-Founder & Board Member

Co-founder of GAZHP and the visionary who sparked the movement in 2019. A specialist in Internal Medicine and Cardiology based in Canada, and President of Dr David Kazhila Medicine Professional Corporation.

LinkedIn
Dr. John Mukuka Musonda
Acting Board Chair

A specialist family physician (PhD, M.Med, MBA, MSc) who heads a Family Medicine unit for Gauteng Health and serves on the joint academic staff of the University of the Witwatersrand, Johannesburg. Extensive clinical, research and administrative experience across district, regional and tertiary hospitals.

LinkedIn
Dr. Sebastian Benaya
Interim Secretary

General Cardiologist and Internal Medicine Specialist at Fredericton Chalmers Regional Hospital, and Assistant Clinical Professor of Medicine at Dalhousie University, Canada.

Dr. Pierre George Dil
Assistant Secretary

Yareka M'hango

CPA Ali J. Mweene
