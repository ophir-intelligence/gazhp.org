Join the Alliance

GAZHP welcomes Zambian healthcare professionals, students, and supporters from every corner of the globe. Find the membership that fits your role and commitment.

Why Join GAZHP

Be Part of Something Bigger

As a member, you join a global community of Zambian healthcare professionals dedicated to transforming healthcare at home — while advancing your own professional growth.

Global Professional Network

Connect with 100+ Zambian healthcare professionals across 15+ countries — physicians, nurses, researchers, and allied health professionals.

Continuous Medical Education

Access training programs, webinars, mentorship, and CME opportunities designed for both diaspora and locally-based Zambian professionals.

Direct Impact on Zambia

Your membership directly funds and supports programs that improve healthcare delivery for millions of Zambians across the country.

Leadership Opportunities

Take on leadership roles in programs, committees, and initiatives. GAZHP is member-driven and there are always opportunities to lead and contribute.

FAQ

Membership Questions

Do I need to be Zambian to join?

Professional and Student membership is open to all Zambian health professionals — doctors, nurses, administration, technical and support staff — as well as Zambian students in advanced programs. Corporations can join as Corporate Members, and others who wish to collaborate can join as Sponsors & Partners.

I'm based in Zambia, can I still join?

Absolutely! GAZHP welcomes both diaspora and locally-based Zambian healthcare professionals. Local members are essential to everything we do.

Are membership fees tax-deductible?

GAZHP is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Please consult your tax advisor regarding the deductibility of membership fees in your country of residence.

How do I apply?

Click the "Apply Now" button on your chosen membership tier, or visit our Contact page and select "Membership Application" as your inquiry type. Our team will reach out within 5 business days.
