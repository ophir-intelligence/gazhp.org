Who We Are

A Global Alliance Born from a Single Conversation

GAZHP connects Zambian healthcare professionals living abroad with those at home — creating a powerful bridge of expertise, mentorship, and resources to uplift Zambia's entire health system.

✓
Diaspora-Led, Locally Grounded Our members span multiple countries but keep Zambia at the center of every initiative.
✓
Healthcare Professionals Driving Solutions Doctors, nurses, and allied health professionals — not just donors — lead our programs.
✓
Focus on Sustainability We build capacity so Zambia becomes self-sufficient, not dependent on external aid.
Founded in 2019 after Dr. David Kazhila was asked on a radio program what physicians could contribute to Zambia. By December 2019, GAZHP was formally registered as a nonprofit.

Core Programs

Where We Focus Our Efforts

Our three flagship programs target the areas of greatest need in Zambia's health landscape, combining diaspora expertise with local partnerships.

Program 01
Addiction & Mental Health

Promoting awareness and culturally relevant mental health services through local and diaspora partnerships. Breaking the stigma and building accessible, compassionate care across Zambia.

Program 02
Cardiovascular Care

Strengthening heart care through clinical training, specialist mentorship, telemedicine consultations, and partnerships with global cardiology experts.

Program 03
Family Medicine & Primary Care

Building the backbone of Zambia's health system through training, research, and international collaboration. Strong primary care for every community.

Our Approach

Four Pillars of Impact

Everything we do flows through four core functions that ensure sustainable, accountable, and ethically driven healthcare transformation.

🤝

Collaborate

Connect diaspora professionals with local practitioners and communities, building bridges of knowledge and care that span continents.

📚

Build Capacity

Strengthen clinical skills through training, mentorship, and continuous medical education — empowering Zambia's own healthcare workforce.

💡

Innovate

Leverage technology, telemedicine, and research for improved healthcare delivery — bringing cutting-edge solutions to underserved communities.

🚑

Support Care Delivery

Mobilize resources and partnerships for frontline services, ensuring that life-saving care reaches patients in the most remote areas.

Why GAZHP

What Makes Us Different

We are not a traditional aid organization. We are healthcare professionals taking ownership of our homeland's health future.

🌍

Diaspora-Led, Locally Grounded

Our members live abroad but our roots — and our focus — remain firmly in Zambia. We understand both worlds.

⚕️

Healthcare Professionals Driving Solutions

We don't just fund programs — we design, lead, and implement them with clinical expertise and professional accountability.

🔄

Sustainability Over Dependency

Every project is designed to build lasting capacity. We empower Zambia's own workforce rather than creating reliance on external support.

🤲

Accountable, Ethical, Collaborative

As a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit, we operate with full transparency, ethical governance, and deep community collaboration.
